Ingredients

in one bowl:


1 cup brown sugar
2/3 cup molasses
6 tablespoons butter

1 tbsp. baking soda
1 tbsp. ginger (or more, as desired)
1 cup sour milk

Instructions

1) mix together the sugar, molasses, and shortening.
2) add sour milk and mix.
3) stir in dry ingredients.
4) put cookie dough on a cookie sheet and bake for 20-30 minutes for 350 degrees F
