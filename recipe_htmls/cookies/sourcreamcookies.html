Sour Cream Sugar Cookies

1/2 cup of butter
1 1/2 cup of sugar
1 cup of sour cream
1 tsp vanilla
2 eggs
4 1/2 cups sifted flour
2 tsps baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
Cream the butter and sugar. Add vanilla and eggs, then mix well. Sift together dry ingredients. add alternatly with sour cream mixing well after each addition. Drop by spoon onto a greased cooking sheet. Take a glass and dip it in water, then sugar. Press glass onto each cookie to flatten them. Repeat as needed. Bake at 375ยบ for 8 minutes.
