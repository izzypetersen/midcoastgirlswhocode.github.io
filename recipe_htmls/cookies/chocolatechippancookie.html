Chocolate Chip Pan Cookies

PAM cooking spray
2 1/4 cup of flour
1 tsp of baking soda
1 tsp of salt
1 cup of softened butter
3/4 cup sugar
3/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
2 eggs
1 pkg of chocolate chips
1 cup of chopped nuts

Preheat oven to 375ยบF Spray 15"x10"x1" baking pan with PAM. In a small bowl combine flour, baking soda, and salt, then set aside. In a large bowl combine butter, sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract. Beat until creamy. Beat in eggs. Gradually add flour mixture then mix well. Stir in chocolate chips and add nuts. Spread into baking pan. Bake at 375ยบ for 20-25 minutes. Cool then cut into 2" squares.
