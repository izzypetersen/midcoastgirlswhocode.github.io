Peanut Butter Pan Cookies

Vegetable cooking spray
2 cups of flour
3/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 cup sugar
1 egg
1 pkg. Peanut Butter Chips
1/2 tsp vanilla
1 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
Preheat oven to 375ยบ. Spray a 15"x 10"x 1" baking pan. In a small bowl combine flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside and in a seperate larger bowl, combine butter, brown sugar, sugar, and vanilla. Beat until creamy. Add egg, beat well. Gradually mix in flour mixture. Stir in Peanut butter chips, spread into a baking pan.
