Cherry Fudge Cookies

2 squares baking chocolate
1/4 cup cooking oil
1 cup of sugar
2 unbeaten eggs
1 tsp of vanilla
1 cup of flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt

Melt the chocolate and mix with the cooking oil. Then stir in sugar, eggs, and vanilla. Afterwards, sift together flour, baking powder, and salt. Chill for an hour. Then after dampening your hands form into little balls, roll in powdered sugar and bake at 400ยบ for ten minutes
