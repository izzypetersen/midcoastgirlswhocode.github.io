No Time Chocolate Graham Fingers

1/2 cup butter
1/2 cup brown sugar
1 egg (beaten)
3/4 cup of walnuts
2 tablespoon Cocoa
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 lb graham crackers (about 20)

Melt butter in pan, add sugar, cocoa, and egg. Bring to a boil, and add vanilla, chopped nuts and coarsely broken crackers. Pour into greased shallow pan and press down with spoon. Ice with chocolate confectioners icing. When cold, cut into fingers. You can also cut into fingers and roll in confectioners sugar.
