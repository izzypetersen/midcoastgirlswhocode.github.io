Orange Fruit Cookies

2/3 cup ground orange and juice
1/2 cup shortening
3/4 cup of sugar
2 egg yolks
1 1/4 cup sifted flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
2 teaspoons grated orange peel
Cut oranges(unpeeled) and blend them. You could also just buy juice with a ton of pulp. Cream sugar and shortening together. Add beaten egg yolks. Sift flour, baking powder, salt and add to creamed mixture alternatly with ground orange and juice. Drop by spoonful onto a greased cookie sheet. Sprinkle with grated orange peel. Bake in a 350ยบ oven for about 15 minutes.
