Cole Slaw Dressing

Ingredients


1/4 cup mayonaise
1/4 tsp. salt
2 tsp. sugar
2 tbsp. cream
2 tsp.mustard
1 tsp. vinegar
3 cups shredded cabbage

Instructions

1) Combine mayonaise, salt, sugar, cream, mustard and vinegar.
2) Blend well and chill
3) When ready to serve, pour dressing over cabbage and toss lightly
