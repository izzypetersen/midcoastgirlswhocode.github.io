Whoopie Pies

2 3/4 cup of flour
1 teaspoon of baking soda
1 teaspoon of baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup hot water
1/2 cup cocoa
1/2 cup butter
1 1/2 cups of sugar
1 tsp vanilla
2 eggs
1/2 cup milk

             Mix butter and sugar. Add Eggs; add hotwater to cocoa DO NOT DRINK. Add dry
    ingredients alternately with milk add vanilla. Drop mixture by teaspoon onto
    the baking sheet. Bake at 350ยบ

Filling

1/2 cup of milk
2 1/2 tablespoons of flour
1/2 cup of sugar
1/2 cup of shortening
1 tablespoon of vanilla
Pinch of salt
1 tablespoon of marshmellow fluff(optional)
              Cook together milk and flour, then cool. Beat sugar with shortening. Add 
    vanilla and salt. Combine mixtures. If you want you can add marshmellow
    fluff.
