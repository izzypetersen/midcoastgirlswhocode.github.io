Apple Lemon Country Cake

1 cup of sugar
1 egg
1/4 cup of softened butter
1 cup of flour
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon soda
3 cups peeled and thinly sliced apples
1 cup of chopped nuts
               In a 3-quart mixer bowl, combine sugar, egg, and butter; beat at a 
  medium speed until light and creamy. Add remaining ingriedents except for the 
  apples and nuts. With a spoon stir in apples and nuts. Pour the mixture into a 
  greased 9 inch square pan. Bake near the center of the oven at 350ยบ for 30-40 
  minutes or until the top springs back when gently poked.Serve with whipped cream.
