Cupcakes

1/2 cup soft butter
6 tablespoons of sugar
6 tablespoons of brown sugar
1/2 tablespoon vanilla
1 egg
1 cup and 2 tablespoons flour
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt 
		       Beat together the butter, sugar, brownsugar, and vanilla. Then beat in 
	the egg. Then stir in the flour, baking powder, and salt. Poir mixture into muffin
	tins lined with cupcake liners. Bake at 375ยบ for 10-12 minutes.

Topping

1/2 cup firm brown sugar
1 egg
A dash of salt
1 big package of chocolate chips
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 teaspoon vanilla 
 
					Combine brown sugar, egg, and salt until thick. Then mix in 
	chocolate chips and walnuts. Place about one tablespoon of frosting on each 
	cupcake.
