Raisin cake

2 cups of sugar
4 cups of flour
2 1/2 cups of water
2 tablespoons of lard
1 package of seedless raisins
1 teaspoon of salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon cloves
1 teaspoon nutmeg
2 teaspoons of baking soda 
 
                Bring water to a boil and mix in sugar, lard, and rasins, 
     then cool for five minutes. In a seperate bowl combine the salt, 
     cinnamon, cloves, baking powder, nutmeg and flour. Add 
     to raisin mixture and stir thouroughly. Pour into two bread pans and
