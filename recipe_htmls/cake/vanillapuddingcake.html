Vanilla Pudding Cake

1 package of instant vanilla pudding
1 package of vanilla cake mix
4 eggs
1 cup of sour cream
2/3 cup of oil
1 small package of chocolate chips
1/2 cup of walnuts
            Put all the ingredients in a large bowl and mix at a high speed for ten 
    minutes. Pour oe third of the cake batter in an ungreased angel food pan.
    Sprinkle with chocolate bits and walnuts. Repeat, ending with bits and walnuts 
    on top. Bake for one hour at 350 degrees. Allow one hour for cooling time in pan
    Do not turn upside down when done.
