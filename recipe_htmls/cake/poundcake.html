Pound Cake

1 cup of brown sugar
2 sticks of butter
4 eggs
1 teaspoon of vanilla
1 3/4 cups of flour
1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
    Beat together sugar and butter until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time 
beating well after each addition. Stir in vanilla. Combine flour, baking powder
and salt then stir into the creamed mixture. Pour into a greased loaf pan bake
at 350ยบ for one hour until cake is done.
