California Walnut Pie

Ingredients


1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1-1/4 cups light corn syrup
3 tablespoons butter or margarine
1/4 teaspoon salt
3 eggs
1-1/2 teaspoons vanilla
1 unbaked 9-inch pie shell
1 cup large pieces walnuts

Instructions

1) Mix the brown sugar and flour in saucepan.
2) Add corn syrup, butter and salt and warm over low heat until butter has melted
3) Beats eggs with vanilla
4) Stir in sugar mixture
5) Turn into pie shell and sprinkle with walnuts
6) Bake below oven center at 375 degrees fahrenheit for 40 to 45 minutes or until filling is set in center
7) Cool before cutting
