Apple Pie

Ingredients


1 plain pastry
6 to 8 tart apples
3/4 cup granulated or brown sugar
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. cinnamon or nutmeg, or both
2 tbsp. butter

Instructions

1)Line pie plate (9 inch) with pastry
2) Fill with apples pared and sliced thin
3) Dot with butter
4) Moisten edge of crust, cover with top crust and press edges together
5) Brush crust with milk or cream and bake in hot over (450°F)
6) After 10 minutes then reduce heat to (350°F) and bake 40 to 50 minutes longer
