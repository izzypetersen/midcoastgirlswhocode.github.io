Baked Indian Pudding

Ingredients


1 cup yellow cornmeal
1/2 cup black molasses
1/4 cup granulated sugar
1/4 cup butter
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp baking soda
2 eggs
1 1/2 quarts hot milk

Instructions

1) Miz together cornmeal, molasses, sugar, butter, salt, soda, and eggs with half the milk.
2) Pour into a greased souffle dish or baking pan and bake until mixture comes to a boil.
3) Stir in remaining hot milk and bake at 375 degrees 3 1/2 - 4 hours.
