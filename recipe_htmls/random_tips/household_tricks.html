Household Hints

1) For badly stained hands from gardening, add a tsp sugar to the soapy lather you wash them in

2) Bee Sting -- apply a slice of onion to a bee sting, it will stop the pain and swelling.

3) Jewelry -- Tooth paste on an old tooth brush is excellent to clean jewelry

4) Nuts -- To get walnut meats out of whole nuts, soak the nuts on night in salt water before you crack them.

5) Eggs -- To keep eggs from cracking when you boil them in the shell, add a teaspoon salt to the water.
