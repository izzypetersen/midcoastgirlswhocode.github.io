Poultry Seasoning

2 T. dried marjoram 2 T. dried parsley 2 T. dried savory 1 T. dried sage 1 1/2 tsp. dried thyme 
             Blend all ingredients thouroughly and store in an airtight 
container away from light. Use to season on poultry dressing or with 
baked broiled or oven fried chicken or turkey.
