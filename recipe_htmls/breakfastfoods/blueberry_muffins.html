Blueberry Muffins

Makes one dozen

Ingredients


2 cups flour
1/2 tsp salt
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 cup softened butter
1 cup sugar
3 large eggs
1/2 cup milk
1 tsp vanilla
3 cups blueberries

Instructions

1) With the mixer on low speed, cream the butter and sugar together until fluffy.
2) Add eggs, one at a time, and mix until well blended.
3) Combine the dry ingredients in a seperate bowl, and add to the mixture, alternating with the milk.
4) Add the vanilla.
5) Fold in the blueberries by hand until well mixed into the batter.
6) Grease the muffin tin well, including the top.
7) Pile the batter high in each muffin cup
8) Sprinkle each generously with sugar.
9) Bake at 375 degrees F for 25-30 minutes.
10) Cool in the pan for 30 minutes before serving
