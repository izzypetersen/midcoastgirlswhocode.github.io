Pickled Eggs

12 Eggs
6 red beets, or more if desired (reserve juice)
Salt, pepper
1 cup of vinegar
            Hard-boil eggs. Boil several red beets, adding 1/2 teaspoon salt 
and 1/2 teaspoon pepper. Drain the juice into jar and cool. Add vinegar 
and season with salt and pepper. Place hard-boiled eggs in a jar and left
stand for an hour before serving. Makes 12 servings.
