Scalloped Scallops

Choice number of Scallops
2 cups of white sauce
1/2 teaspoon sugar
Pinch of nutmeg
1 tablespoon of butter
1 1/2 cups of cracker crumbs
    Cook scallops with white sauce sugar and nutmeg. Then butter a 
small casserole dish and put the mixture in the dish. Then cover 
with cracker crumbs. Bake at 400ยบ for 15-20 minutes.
