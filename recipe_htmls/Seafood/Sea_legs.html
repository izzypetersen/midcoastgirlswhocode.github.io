Sea Leg and Shrimp Souffle

16 slices of bread (trimmed and buttered)
8 slices of cheese
5 eggs
3 cups of milk
1 lb of shrimp, cooked or raw
1/2 lb of sea legs 
        Take a shallow baking dish grease well. Place 1/2 the bread slices on 
the bottom and side. Place all of the cheese. Cover with fish. Place the
rest of the bread on top. Beat egg and milk together pour over all. Top 
generously with parmesan cheese. Let stand overnight if you wish. Bake 
325 degrees until all puffed and brown.
