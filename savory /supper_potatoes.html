Supper Potatoes

Ingredients


cold, boiled potatoes
1/2 cup milk
4 tbsp butter
salt and pepper
parsley
juice from half a lemon

Instructions

1) Slice potatoes about 1/4 inch thick
2) Put potatoes in frying pan with milk
3) Add butter, salt, and pepper
4) Cook slowly until heated thoroughly
5) Add parsley and lemon juice
